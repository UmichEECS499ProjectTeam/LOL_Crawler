Marks
The
12
Types
of
Marks
Marks
are
the
red
runes
.
They
have
the
physical
offensive
and
magical
penetration
boosts
as
their
primary
runes.
Their
primary
runes
are
very
useful
to
DPS
champions,
as
well
as
providing
magic
penetration
for
mages.
There
are
no
Marks
of
Scaling
Cooldown
Reduction,
Scaling
Mana
Regeneration,
Scaling
Armor,
Scaling
Health
Regeneration,
and
Health
Regeneration.
There
are
19
variants
of
marks.
The
Marks
are
represented
in
four
forms:
two
crossed
arrows,
a
potion,
a
dagger,
and
a
spiked
mace.
