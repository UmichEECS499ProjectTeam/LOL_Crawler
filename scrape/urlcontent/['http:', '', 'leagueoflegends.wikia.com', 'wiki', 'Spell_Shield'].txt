Spell
shields
refer
to
one
of
several
effects
that
have
the
capacity
to
negate
enemy
abilities.
Single
ability
The
following
will
block
one
enemy
ability,
with
precedence
given
to
the
ability
shield.
Banshee's
Veil
Sivir's
Spell
Shield
Nocturne's
Shroud
of
Darkness
Other
Morgana's
Black
Shield
which
instead
of
negating
one
ability,
absorbs
magic
damage
and
makes
the
champion
immune
to
crowd
control
.
