The
Wraith
is
a
neutral
monster
in
League
of
Legends
described
in
game
as
"A
fragile
monster
that
takes
less
damage
from
physical
attacks
and
steals
life
each
time
it
strikes".
The
Wraith
only
had
life
steal
prior
to
patch
V1.0.0.130
.
The
dead
still
plague
the
aftermath
of
the
Summoner's
Rift
and
Twisted
Treeline
,
and
those
who
chose
to
linger
here
have
turned
to
ghastly
Wraiths.
These
flying
Undead
attack
any
who
happen
to
pass
by,
haunting
their
victims
with
visions
of
the
death
and
destruction
that
took
place
here.
In
Summoner's
Rift
it
will
also
have
a
buff
which
returns
health.
It
can
be
found
amongst
three
Lesser
Wraiths
on

Lesser

.
