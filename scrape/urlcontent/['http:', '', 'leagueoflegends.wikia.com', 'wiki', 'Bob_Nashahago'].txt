Bob
Nashahago
is
a
summoner
and
journalist.
He
works
as
reporter
for
The
Journal
of
Justice
.
Trivia
His
tag-line
is
"I'm
Bob
Nashahago
and
you're
not".
He
is
dating
Nidalee
.
He
gets
many
threatening
letters
because
of
jealous
fans.
Janna
was
once
matched
through
Blitzcrank's
dating
service
with
the
summoner
and
journalist
Bob
Nashahago.
It
is
unknown
if
they
intended
to
date
one
another,
considering
that
Nashahago
had
already
been
dating
Nidalee.
