The
Offense
Mastery
Tree
is
one
of
the
three
Mastery
branches.
All
masteries
were
redesigned
on
November
15,
2011.
The
information
from
this
article
refers
to
the
version
of
this
mastery
tree
used
before
the
redesign.
Focus
Its
main
focus
was
to
give
advantage
to
offensive
actions
like
physical
damage,
magic
damage
,
critical
strike,
armor
penetration
,
among
other
buffs
.
The
Summoner
Spells
Exhaust
(Tier
1),
Smite
(Tier
1)
Ignite
(Tier
3)
and
Rally
(Tier
5)
are
improved
by
this
Mastery
.
It
would
take
31
points
to
max
the
Offense
Mastery
out.
This
mastery
tree
benefits
AD
carries
the
most,
while
also
providing
useful
benefits
for
casters.
