to

to

while

,

Use

and

of

combined

for

exceptionally
well

combo
in

then

the

using

.
Rengar
can
hop
over

using
Unseen
Predator
.
Place
a
ward

a
jungle

and

a

over

However,

Also,
when
your
Bonetooth
Necklace
reaches
9
Trophies,
use
the
extra
range
on
your
passive
to

this
wall-hopping
easier.
This
is
a
great
tactic
for
stealing
epic
monsters
when
counter
jungling
or
killing
the
enemy
jungler.
A
great
way
to
reach
high
amounts
of
attack
speed
very
fast
and
burst
down
enemies
is
to
use
your
Savagery
and
then
your
Empowered
Savagery
right
after
(or
the
vice
versa
).
Because
the
attack
speed
bonuses
stack
additively
this
provides
90%
AS
at
1
st
rank
and
150%
AS
at
5
th
rank.
Whenever
Rengar
turns
invisible
using
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
,
he
gains
a
point
of
Ferocity
every
0.75
seconds.
Keep
that
in
mind
because
it
can
turn
the
tide
of
a
battle,
increasing
one
of
three
things
about
Rengar:
his
damage,
his
utility
or
his
self-healing
ability.
Rengar's
Savagery
and
Empowered
Savagery
allow
him
to
take
down
turrets
very
quickly
and
with
the
added
survivability
from
Battle
Roar
make
him
exceptionally

at
backdooring
.
He
is
capable
of
great
pushes
both
early
and
late
game
so
make
sure
to
use
this
to
your
advantage
by
pushing
the
turrets
early
on
to
get
bonus
gold
for
your
team.
Maxing
Savagery
first
gives
you
great
attack
speed
,
single
targeted
damage
and
burst
.
Some
might
argue
that
Savagery
is
a
one-point
wonder
skill,
as
its
scaling
is
based
on
Rengar's
total
attack
damage
and
thus
remains
powerful
even
at
Rank
1.
It
is
also
difficult
to
keep
opponents
in
range
to
fully
utilize
the
bonus
attack
speed,
unless
allied
crowd
control
abilities
are
strong
enough
to
keep
foes
locked
down.
Rengar
is
a
great
jungler
as
his
Empowered
Battle
Roar
heals
him
for
a
flat
amount
of
his
health.
This
allows
him
for
excellent
survivability
both
in
lanes
and
jungles.
When
jungling
alternate
between
Empowered
Battle
Roar
and
Empowered
Savagery
.
The
sustain
of
Empowered
Savagery
with
a
Vampiric
Scepter
is
better
than
Empowered
Battle
Roar
if
you
can
do
full
damage
with
the
stacked
attack
speed.
Use
this
to
your
advantage
to
get
faster
clear
times,
and
get
life
back.
Maxing
Battle
Roar
first
gives
you
a
good
burst
of
AoE
damage,
a
high
defense
boost
for
a
few
seconds
and
allows
for
generating
ferocity
faster
with
cooldown
going
down.
Consider
it
over
maxing
Savagery
,
when
jungling
or
playing
defensively.
Use
Rengar's
Empowered
Bola
Strike
in
order
to
stop
enemies
from
running
away.
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
is
a
decent
tool
for

ganks
and
other

situations.
However
consider
that
the
stealth
can
be
delayed
to
up
to
3
seconds
when
attacked.
Rengar
can
use
his
ultimate
in
order
to
see
invisible
champions,
such
as
Shaco
,
Twitch
,
Evelynn
,
or
another
Rengar
.
When

cast
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
Rengar
does

immediately
turn
invisible
but
after
about
a
second
delay.
So
keep
this
in
mind
while

are
in

Rengar's
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
allows
him
to
scout
as
it
reveals
enemy

in
a
huge

This

allow
his

to
catch
enemy
champions
hiding
in
bushes,
or
to
prevent
an
ambush
by
the
enemy
hiding
in
bushes.
This
can
also
prevent
enemy
champions
juking
you
or
your
allies
using
the
fog
of
war
or

When
ganking
a
lane
warded
with
Sight
Ward
,
use
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
outside
of
the
ward's
range.
Item/skill
usage
ends
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
,
but
with
the
half
second
delay
Rengar
can
still

This
is

to
finish
off
almost

with
Ravenous
Hydra/Blade
of

King
and

Rengar

and

and

Therefore,

Necklace

At

18

41
AD
(2

for

800
gold
and
should
stay
a
part
of

unless

spot

for

later

Bonetooth
Necklace
should

later

-

.
Sword
of

Divine
can

In

Edge
and

can

Youmuu's
Ghostblade
Is

Youmuu's

With

,

from

,

CDR

Spirit

,

You

Locket

,

,

.
The

With

and

,

The

Mallet
allow

The

the

,

,

Rengar
has

(

).

,

.

Countering
Since
Rengar
deals
mostly
physical
damage,
buying
armor
is
a
top
priority.
Ninja
Tabi
and
Thornmail
are
very
effective
against
him
since
the
majority
of
his
damage
comes
from
autoattacks
enhanced
with
Savagery
.
Avoid
fighting
Rengar
near
and
especially
inside
brushes
as
his
passive
Unseen
Predator
provides
him
with
great
mobility
and
utility.
His
passive
has
no
cooldown
making
slows
ineffective
when
fighting
him
inside
brushes
as
he
can
constantly
close
the
distance.
Skilled
players
can
use
Unseen
Predator
to
juke
and
escape
when
being
chased
and
even
dodge
skillshots
and
avoid
AoEs
.
Avoid
fighting
Rengar
1
on
1
as
his
kit
makes
him
an
exceptionally
good
duelist.
Battle
Roar
and
Empowered
Battle
Roar
make
him
deceptively
tanky.
That
alongside
life
steal
and
Savagery
allow
Rengar
to
quickly
regain
health
and
with
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
and
Empowered
Savagery
to
overpower
you
even
if
he
was
at
low
health
when
you
started
fighting.
Avoid
engaging
Rengar
when
he
already
has
2
or
more
Ferocity,
as
when
he
uses
all
3
of
his
basic
abilities
he
will
be
able
to
cast
one
Empowered
spell
as
well.
Beware
if
Rengar
is
jungling
as
he
has
great
ganking
potential
-
in
addition
to
his
high
damage
output
and
burst
capabilities,
Bola
Strike
and
Phage
or
Frozen
Mallet
prevent
his
target
from
escaping
while
Unseen
Predator
and
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
make
sure
he
can
chase
down
his
target
even
if
they
posses
a
short
term
stealth
or
use
Flash
.
If
Rengar
uses
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
while
your
team
has
sight
of
him
a
distinctive
sound
can
be
heard.
Otherwise
you
will
receive
no
warning
of
the
abilityâ€™s
use.
This
also
works
if
you
are
in
sight
of
a
bush
that
he
uses
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
in.
Rengar
relies
heavily
on
perfect
surprise
attacks
and
burst
to
take
down
his
target,
stunning
him
and
getting
out
of
melee
range
will
stop
his
momentum
and
ruin
his
intial
attack.
Placing
Vision
Ward
near
your
lane
can
prevent
Rengar
from
ganking
you
using
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
to
bypass
a
Sight
Ward
.
Placing
a
Vision
Ward
or
using
a
Oracle's
Elixir
when
Rengar
uses
Thrill
of
the
Hunt
to
run
can
prevent
his
stealthy
escape.
Traps
such
as
Noxious
Trap
can
punish
him
for
entering
bushes,
hindering
his
ability
to
leap
at
you.
Additionally,
he
is
particularly
susceptible
to
Blinds,
as
almost
all
of
his
damage
is
autoattack
reliant.
He
doesn't
trade
very
well,
so
if
you
can
keep
him
at
a
distance
and
poke
him
constantly
you
will
greatly
increase
your
chances
of
success.
