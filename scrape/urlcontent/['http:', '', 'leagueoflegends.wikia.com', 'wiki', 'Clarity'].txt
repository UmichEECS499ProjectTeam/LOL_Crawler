Clarity
Clarity
Effect
Restores
40%
maximum
mana
to
you
and
nearby
allies.
Cooldown
180
Range
Self,
with
AoE
of
600
Breaks
stealth
Yes
Level
6
Example
Clarity
is
a
'non-targeted'
Summoner
Spell.
This
spell
instantly
restores
a
portion
of
mana
for
yourself
and
all
nearby
allied
champions.
Strategy
Clarity
is
best
taken
in
teams
where
most,
if
not
all,
champions
are
mana
based.
Usually
this
spell
is
taken
by
a
mana-hungry
champion
that
needs
to
compensate
with
low
base
mana/mana
regen
(eg.
Malphite
),
or
focuses
on
aggressive
play
by
spamming
their
skills
(eg.
Ryze
).
