Quintessense
The
12
Types
of
Quintessences
Quintessences
are
the
gold/purple
runes
.
They
have
superior
power
and
stats
to
all
of
the
glyphs
,
seals
and
marks
,
as
well
as
offering
some
stats
that
cannot
be
found
in
any
of
the
other
three
rune
types.
One
quintessence
slot
is
unlocked
at
level
10,
20,
and
30
for
a
total
of
three.The
number
of
variants
of
quintessences
is
33.
Quintessences
are
represented
in
four
forms:
a
dragon,
a
ram,
a
lion,
and
a
tree.
