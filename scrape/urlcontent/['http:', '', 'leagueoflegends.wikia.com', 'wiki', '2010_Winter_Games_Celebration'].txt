Banner

2010.
The
2010
Winter
Games
Celebration
was
an
event
to
celebrate
the
2010
Winter
Olympics
.
Riot
introduced
limited
edition
Winter
Games
skins
and
runes,
as
well
as
a
Winter
Games-themed
variation
of
the
winter
map.
The
event
began
on
February
12
and
was
ended
on
March
2.
