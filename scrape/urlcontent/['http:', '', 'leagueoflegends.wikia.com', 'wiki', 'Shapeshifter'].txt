Shapeshifter
refers
to
a
champion
with
multiple
forms.
Champions
possessing
two
set
of
skills
for
each
of
their
forms:
Elise's
Spider
Form
Ranged
Mage
/
Melee
Assassin
with
two
sets
of
basic
abilities
.
Jayce's
Transform:
Mercury
Cannon
and
Transform:
Mercury
Hammer
Ranged
Carry
/
Melee
Fighter
with
two
sets
of
basic
abilities
.
Nidalee's
Aspect
of
the
Cougar
Ranged
Support
/
Melee
Pusher
with
one
set
of
basic
abilities
and
one
set
of
ultimate
abilities
.
Quinn's
Tag
Team
and
Skystrike
Ranged
Carry
/
Melee
Carry
with
one
set
of
basic
abilities
that
change
function
and
an
alternating
ultimate
ability
.
Champions
with
form-changing
abilities:
Kayle's
Righteous
Fury
enhances
her
autoattacks,
granting
them
bonus
magic
damage,
area
of
effect
on
non-tower
targets
and
making
her
a
ranged
champion
for
the
duration.
Nasus's
Fury
of
the
Sands
empowers
him,
granting
him
bonus
health
and
area
of
effect
damage
that
increases
his
attack
damage.
Renekton's
Dominus
empowers
him,
granting
him
bonus
health,
area
of
effect
damage
and
passive
fury
generation.
Shyvana's
Dragon's
Descent
empowers
her,
enhancing
each
of
her
abilities.
Swain's
Ravenous
Flock
empowers
him,
granting
him
multi-target
damage
and
healing.
Udyr's
stances
change
his
appearance.
