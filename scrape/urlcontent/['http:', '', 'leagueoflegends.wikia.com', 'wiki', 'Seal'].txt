Seals
are
the
yellow
runes
.
Primary
seals
grant
boosts
to
a
champion's
defense,
these
are
very
useful
to
tanks.
There
are
no
Seals
of
Scaling
Cooldown
Reduction,
Armor
Penetration
or
Magic
Penetration.
There
are
24
variants
of
seals.
The
Seals
are
represented
in
three
forms:
a
chalice,
a
shield,
and
a
wing.
