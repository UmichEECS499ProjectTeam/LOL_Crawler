The

"Observer"
Observer
was
a
summoner
spell
only
available
to
Riot
Staff.
Upon
activation,
the
fog
of
war
is
cleared
and
all

may
be
seen.
This
effect
only
applies
to
the
user
who
activated
the
spell.
The
summoner
skill
is
no
longer
available
in
any
realm,
instead
replaced
by
Spectator
Mode
.

"Observer"
