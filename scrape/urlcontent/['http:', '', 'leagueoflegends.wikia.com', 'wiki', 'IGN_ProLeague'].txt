IPL
5
Format
IGN
invites
one
team
to
the
League
of
Legends
tournament
and
locates
fourteen
other
teams
from
online
qualifiers
and
one
additional
team
as
a
Wildcard
qualifier
event.
Four
teams
are
put
in
four
groups
in
a
bracket
format.
Double
elimination
rules
apply
and
the
two
winners
of
each
bracket
move
on
to
the
next
round.
The
final
bracket
games
are
all
decided
by
best
of
three,
while
the
championship
match
is
best
of
five.
