Season
Two
is
the
second
competitive
time
period
for
League
of
Legends
.
Season
Two
began
on
November
29,
2011,
a
week
after
the
release
of
patch
V1.0.0.130
.
The
patch
introduced
the
champion
Volibear
,
a
new
set
of
masteries
,
the
removal
of
the
summoner
spells
Fortify
and
Rally
,
and
the
addition
of
Surge
and
Promote
for
Classic
Mode
.
The
season
was
scheduled
to
conclude
on
6
November
2012,
[1]
but
was
extended
and
officially
concluded
on
12
November
2012.
[2]
