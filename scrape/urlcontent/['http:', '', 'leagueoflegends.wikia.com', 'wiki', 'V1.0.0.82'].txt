Kennen,
the
Heart
of

Tempest
Kennen
is
a
new
magical
assassin
ninja,
using
the
energy
system
to
cast
his
spells
instead
of
mana.
Thundering
Shuriken
:
Kennen
throws
a
lightning

towards
a

damaging
the
first

it

and

a
Mark
of

Storm

Electrical
Surge
:
Kennen
passively

extra

and

Mark

Storm
to

every

and

this

to

and
add

Lightning
Rush
:
Kennen

into
a

of

receiving
a
massive
energy
boost
and

to
move
through
units.
Enemy

he
passes
through
receive

and
gain
a
Mark
of

Storm.
Slicing
Maelstrom
(Ultimate):
Kennen

to

enemy

Mark
of

Storm
(Innate):
Kennen's
abilities

of
the
Storm
to
opponents
they

which
explode

the
opponent
and
restoring
some
of
Kennen's
energy.
