ability
is

that
must
be
activated
by
the

to
take
effect,
but
once
activated,
provides
a
continuous,
passive
effect
until
deactivated.
The
toggling
of
an
ability
is
not
considered
an
activation

of
Sheen
or
Kassadin's
Force

,
but

to

an
ability
will
