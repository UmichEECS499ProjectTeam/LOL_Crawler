Magic
damage
is
one
of
the
three
types
of
damage
in
League
of
Legends
,
the
others
being
physical
damage
and
true
damage
.
Magic
damage
is
dealt
by
some
items
and
most
champions'
spells,
though
some
spells
deal
physical
or
true
damage.
Statistics
influencing
magic
damage
Most
magic
damage
can
be
increased
with
ability
power
,
or
attack
damage
in
some
rare
cases,
but
some
cannot
be
modified,
such
as
Sunfire
Cape
's
damage
per
second
or
Madred's
Razors
'
effect
against
minions.
The
amount
of
magic
damage
received
is
reduced
by
a
certain
percentage
based
on
the
target's
magic
resistance
.
The
effectiveness
of
magic
resistance
can
be
reduced
with
magic
penetration
.
