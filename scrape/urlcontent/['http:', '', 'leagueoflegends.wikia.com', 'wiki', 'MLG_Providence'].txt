About
MLG
Providence
was
the
second
event
in
the
MLG
Pro
Circuit
to
feature
League
of
Legends.
Thirty-two
teams
competed
in
the
online
qualifiers
for
the
event,
and
the
top
four
teams
were
flown
to
Providence
to
fight
for
the
$15,000
top
prize.
Format
Thirty-two
teams
entered
the
online
qualifiers,
which
took
place
on
November
5th.
Eight
teams
were
invited,
while
the
remaining
24
spots
were
filled
by
open
registration.
It
was
a
single-elimination
bracket,
with
each
match
consisting
of
a
best-of-three
series.
The
top
four
teams
were
flown
out
to
Providence
to
compete
in
the
live
event.
At
MLG
Providence
itself,
the
four
teams
competed
in
a
double-elimination
bracket
with
each
match
once
again
being
a
best-of-three
series.
The
one
exception
was
the
Grand
Finals,
which
were
a
best
of
seven
that
included
the
results
from
the
previous
series
played
by
the
teams.

Pool
The
prize
pool
was
$32,000,

among
the
teams
as
