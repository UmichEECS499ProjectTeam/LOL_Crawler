This is a tribute to one of Riot's employees named Banksy (David Banks, Director of Platform Engineering). The image artwork would seem to be based off of the Sorting Hat from the Harry Potter franchise. There are eyes and a mouth-like band visible on the hat, very similar to the Sorting Hat. Not counting other ability power items , runes , and masteries Rabadon's Deathcap gives 156 ability power alone. The upgrade from its parts grants 36 AP. While it has the potential to be the single highest source of ability power in the game, Rabadon's Deathcap is rarely purchased first, as its passive ability requires other ability power items to be fully effective, to the point of the item being barely cost effective, which the large majority of expensive items are by a large margin. It is also somewhat difficult to build early in the game as one of its components is quite expensive, making it difficult to purchase without kills or other gold income. Skins Safari Caitlyn skin is a reference to not only to (African) safaris , but also to the web browser Safari . She shares the internet browsers theme with Explorer Ezreal , Chrome Rammus , and Foxfire Ahri . Resistance Caitlyn skin looks similar to Natasha Volkova , a soviet commando from the Command & Conquer: Red Alert 3 video game. Arctic Warfare Caitlyn skin might be a reference to the Arctic Warfare series of sniper rifles by Accuracy International. The skin was a special gift for those who bought the PC Gamer US November 2011 Issue in September 2011. The skin was later made avalible for everyone to purchase on December 29, 2011 More clearly, this skin is an homage to the legendary Finnish sniper Simo Hyh, who killed 505 Russian soldiers and officers over a span of 100 days during the Russian invasion of Finland in the winter of 1939-1940. Simo has the highest number of confirmed kills of any sniper in any major war in history. Classic Caitlyn skin resembles Mad Moxxi from the Borderlands series. Relations The Hermit of the Howling Abyss knew Caitlyn's mother. Caitlyn is partners with Vi in the Piltover Sheriff's Department. The two have been chasing after Jinx , a dangerous criminal who has been causing serious problems in Piltover for apparently no reason.
